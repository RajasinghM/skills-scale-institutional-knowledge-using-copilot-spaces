# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

## Project Management Process Overview

OctoAcme's project management relies on clear roles, structured lifecycles, and repeatable workflows to ensure effective delivery and continuous improvement. Key principles include customer-first iteration, transparent communication, and measurable outcomes.

**Core processes include:**

- **Project Initiation** — Define project vision, goals, and stakeholder alignment
- **Planning & Backlog Management** — Structure work, prioritize deliverables, and define success metrics
- **Execution & Tracking** — Manage sprint cycles, monitor progress, and adapt to change
- **Risk Management & Communication** — Identify risks early, communicate transparently across teams
- **Release & Deployment** — Ensure quality releases through testing, staging, and production workflows
- **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement** — Reflect on outcomes, capture lessons learned, and iterate
- **Roles & Personas** — Clarify team responsibilities and stakeholder relationships
